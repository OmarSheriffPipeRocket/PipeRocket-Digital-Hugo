The PipeRocket Technical SEO Approach

We focus on outcomes, not error counts. Each phase builds on the last, and every fix is sequenced by pipeline impact.

Crawl the full picture

We start with a complete technical crawl of your site, paired with server log analysis to see exactly how Googlebot and AI crawlers actually move through it — not how you assume they do.

By the end of this phase we know which pages get crawled, how often, where budget is wasted, and which templates are quietly invisible to search.

Diagnose what’s blocking

We separate the issues that block indexation and rankings from the noise every audit tool generates. Crawl traps, render-blocking, duplicate and thin URLs, broken canonicals, redirect chains, orphan pages.

Then we prioritise — ruthlessly — by impact on the pages that drive pipeline, not by the tool's severity colour.

Fix crawl & indexation

We clean up what Google crawls and indexes: robots and meta directives, canonical logic, XML sitemaps, pagination, parameter handling, and the thin or duplicate URLs eating crawl budget.

The result is a site where the pages that should rank get crawled and indexed, and the ones that shouldn't stop competing for attention.

Architect for authority

We rebuild site structure and internal linking so authority flows to the pages that convert — comparisons, alternatives, category landers — instead of pooling on the homepage and blog.

Clean hierarchies, deliberate internal links, and crawl depth that keeps your money pages a few clicks from home.

Engineer page experience

We profile Core Web Vitals at the template level and ship fixes with your engineers: LCP, CLS, INP, render-blocking resources, image and font delivery, and CDN configuration.

Speed work is sequenced by which pages it unblocks, and delivered as PRs, not a list of vague recommendations.

Make JS crawlable

For React, Next.js, and headless builds, we make sure content rendered by JavaScript is actually visible to crawlers — SSR, dynamic rendering, hydration, and lazy-loaded content that search engines can reach.

If your content only exists after a client-side render, it effectively does not exist to search. We fix that.

Structure for search & AI

We implement and validate structured data — Product, SoftwareApplication, FAQ, Organization, Breadcrumb — so Google earns rich results and AI engines can parse what your pages mean.

Content and entities are modelled so ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews can crawl, understand, and cite your site alongside traditional results.

Monitor & protect

Technical SEO is not a one-time project. We crawl daily, watch indexation and Core Web Vitals, and catch the regressions that ship with every release before they cost you rankings.

Performance is reviewed against rankings and pipeline, so the foundation stays solid as your site and product evolve.

Technical SEO questions, answered

What is a technical SEO agency? +
A technical SEO agency fixes the foundation that lets search engines and AI crawlers find, render, index, and rank your site — crawlability, indexation, site architecture, Core Web Vitals, JavaScript rendering, and structured data — and connects that work to qualified pipeline rather than stopping at an audit PDF.
What does technical SEO include? +
Crawl budget and log-file analysis, indexation and coverage fixes, canonicalisation, XML sitemaps, robots and meta directives, site architecture and internal linking, Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, INP), JavaScript rendering, redirects, hreflang, and structured data — sequenced by impact on the pages that drive revenue.
How is technical SEO different from content SEO? +
Content SEO decides what you say and which queries you target. Technical SEO makes sure search engines can actually crawl, render, index, and trust those pages. The best content in the world earns nothing if it's render-blocked, non-indexed, or buried twelve clicks deep — technical SEO removes those blockers.
How long until technical SEO improves rankings? +
Indexation and crawl fixes can move within days to weeks as Google recrawls. Core Web Vitals and architecture changes compound over weeks to a few months. We prioritise the fixes that unblock pipeline pages first, so the highest-impact wins land early rather than at the end of a long roadmap.
Does technical SEO matter for AI search like ChatGPT and AI Overviews? +
Yes. AI engines crawl and parse your site much like search engines do — if content is render-blocked, poorly structured, or missing schema, they can't reliably understand or cite it. Clean crawlability and structured data are what get you surfaced in AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity answers, not just the blue links.
