How our SaaS content marketing agency turns content into pipeline

Sales calls over keyword tools

Most content marketing agencies open Ahrefs first. We open your sales-call recordings first, the language buyers use, the objections that come up, the questions sales answers every demo. Keywords come out of that, not the other way around.

BOFU first, calendar later

Comparison pages, alternative pages, integration deep-dives, ROI calculators. BOFU surfaces ship in month one because that’s where the demo-bookers live. Generic top-of-funnel blog posts compound underneath, but they don’t lead the calendar.

Conversion in every brief

Every page is briefed with a job, book a demo, claim a tool, start a trial, capture intent. Conversion is decided before a word is written, baked into the structure of the page, never bolted on at the end as a "Book a demo" CTA.

Pipeline over output volume

Output volume is a vanity metric. We measure pipeline contribution by content cluster, demos booked from BOFU pages, and AI-citation share of voice. If a piece doesn’t move one of those, we don’t care that we shipped it on time.

Frequently asked questions

What is a content marketing agency? +
A content marketing agency plans, produces, and distributes content that attracts and converts a defined buyer. The good ones go beyond writing, they connect content to pipeline through buyer research, conversion-led briefs, and distribution that lands content where buyers actually look.
How many content pieces per month do your services include? +
Quality, not quantity. A typical engagement ships 6–10 deeply researched pieces a month, half of them BOFU pages built around buying-stage questions, half of them MOFU / TOFU built for AI citation and topical authority. We don’t take engagements measured purely on output volume.
Do you use AI to write content? +
AI for research, drafting variants, and optimisation. Human writers and strategists for buyer voice, sales-call insight, and the editorial judgment that makes content rank, convert, and earn AI citations. Pure-AI content fails on all three.
How do you measure content marketing ROI? +
Pipeline contribution by content cluster, qualified-opportunity rate by page type, AI-citation share of voice, demos booked from BOFU pages, and CAC payback by buyer journey stage. Sessions and ranking are reported but never lead the conversation.
Do you handle distribution or just production? +
Both. Production without distribution is a content calendar with extra steps. Our content marketing services include LinkedIn / newsletter / community distribution, repurposing across formats, and earned-media outreach to extend reach beyond your owned site.
