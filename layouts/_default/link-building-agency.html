Frequently asked questions

What is a link building agency? +
A link building agency earns backlinks to your site from external publications and platforms. Done well, that means editorial placements, podcast features, expert quotes, and citations on sources both Google and AI engines treat as authoritative — building topical authority and ranking power simultaneously.
Are paid links OK? +
No. Paid links violate Google’s guidelines and increasingly fail to influence AI engines too. Our link building services are 100% editorial — earned placements, expert contributions, podcasts, PR. Slower upfront, dramatically more durable.
How many links per month should we expect? +
Quality, not quantity. A typical engagement targets 8–15 editorial / podcast / expert-quote placements per month from publications relevant to your category. One link from a tier-1 trade publication outweighs 50 from a generic SaaS roundup.
How long does link building take to move rankings? +
Branded mention impact is immediate (good for AI engines and direct referral traffic). Ranking lift from authoritative editorial links typically takes 60–120 days as the link is indexed, contextualised, and weighted against your topical cluster.
PR vs guest posts vs digital PR — what works? +
Editorial placements (data-led PR, expert commentary, podcast features) outperform guest posts on most metrics — trust, authority, AI citation worthiness. Digital PR works when there’s a real story or data point. Guest posts work selectively in tier-1 trade publications. We pick channels based on the placement, not the deliverable count.
