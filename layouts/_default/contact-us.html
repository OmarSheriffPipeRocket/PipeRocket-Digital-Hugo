Frequently asked questions

Who should reach out to PipeRocket? +
B2B SaaS founders, CMOs, or marketing leads who need pipeline — not just traffic or impressions. If your current marketing isn't tied to revenue, that's usually the first thing we fix.
What services can I talk to you about? +
SaaS SEO, SaaS PPC, or a combined growth motion. If you're not sure which one fits, that's fine — most of the first call is figuring out what's actually broken in your funnel.
What is a SaaS SEO agency? +
An agency that specialises in helping SaaS companies grow through organic search — combining technical SEO with an understanding of the SaaS customer journey to attract qualified leads and drive conversions.
What is a SaaS PPC agency? +
An agency that runs paid acquisition for SaaS — Google Ads, LinkedIn, Meta, programmatic — and ties every dollar of spend to qualified pipeline rather than vanity click metrics.
Why does PipeRocket focus on pipeline over rankings or clicks? +
Rankings and clicks don't pay salaries. Pipeline does. We've seen too many SaaS teams celebrate top-10 keywords while their sales team starves — so we measure ourselves on the only number that matters to your board.
