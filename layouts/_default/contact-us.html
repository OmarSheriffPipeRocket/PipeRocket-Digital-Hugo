Your ARR range, your stack (HubSpot, Salesforce, Marketo), and the specific failure mode that brought you here - "MQLs are getting rejected", "attribution is broken", "monthly reporting takes three days". The more specific, the more useful the first reply.

No. Plenty of teams talk to us six to nine months before any engagement. The first call costs nothing and we send a written summary afterward whether or not anything happens next.
Are you a fit for early-stage SaaS? +
We work best with Series A through Series C, $3M to $50M ARR. If you are pre-PMF or pre-seed we will say so on the call and point you to better-fit partners.
