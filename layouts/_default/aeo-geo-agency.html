Cited, not just ranked

Pipeline over rankings

The PipeRocket AEO & GEO approach

Each phase is built around how AI engines retrieve, evaluate, and cite content — not how Google ranked content in 2018.

Audit AI visibility

We measure your current citation share of voice across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini for the buyer questions that matter. Baseline first. Anything we can’t measure, we can’t move.

Map buyer questions

We work with sales to map the actual questions your buyers ask AI assistants — comparison, alternatives, integration, pricing, "is X better than Y for Z". Each becomes a target prompt with a target citation outcome.

Engineer for retrieval

Content gets restructured for AI retrieval — claim-source pairing, factual density, schema, retrieval-friendly headings, citation-grade quotes. AI engines find a clean answer to quote, with your brand attached.

Land in training corpora

AI engines train and retrieve from specific corpora — Reddit, Wikipedia-grade sources, GitHub, podcast transcripts, trade press. We earn placements there, not just on high-DR domains AI engines ignore.

Build trust-signal posture

AI engines weight authoritativeness — author profiles, brand entities, citation graphs, structured data. We strengthen the posture that earns citations: real bios, schema entities, consistent NAP/E-A-T signals across the corpus.

Track citations weekly

We monitor citation share of voice across the AI engines weekly, by buyer question, by competitor. Movements get diagnosed (new training cycle? new retrieval index? new prompt pattern?) and acted on.

Wire AI-led pipeline

Referrers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude get tagged in your CRM. Demos and pipeline from AI-led sessions get tracked separately so you can quantify what AI visibility is actually worth.

Compound vs. Google

Everything we ship for AI engines also strengthens classic SEO — schema, citation graphs, factual density. The investment compounds across both worlds, so you don’t pick a winner.

Frequently asked questions

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)? +
AEO is the practice of getting your brand cited as the answer when buyers ask questions to AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews — instead of optimising for a blue link on page one. It combines retrieval-friendly content engineering, schema, and authoritative distribution.
What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)? +
GEO is the broader discipline of being visible inside generative AI experiences — across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and AI Overviews. AEO is one tactic within GEO; GEO also covers entity recognition, brand mention frequency in training corpora, and citation graph posture.
How is AEO different from traditional SEO? +
SEO optimises for a ranked list of links. AEO optimises for being the cited source inside an answer. The work overlaps (schema, authority, factual content) but the success metric shifts from "rank #1" to "cited by ChatGPT when a buyer asks the question". You need both — but most agencies still only ship one.
Which AI engines do you optimise for? +
ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Microsoft Copilot. Each has a different retrieval architecture and corpus weighting — we tune the work per engine and report citation share of voice across all of them.
How quickly does AEO drive pipeline? +
First citations on long-tail buyer questions appear within 30–60 days of restructuring content. Citation share of voice on competitive head terms takes 90–180 days. AI-led pipeline starts trickling immediately because most teams aren’t even tagging it yet — making it visible in the CRM is half the win.
