## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why look for a Directive Consulting alternative in the first place?

Directive is a strong agency for mid-market and enterprise SaaS that can justify $8K–$25K/mo and benefit from a full Customer Generation framework. Companies look for alternatives when the price floor is too high, when they need a different methodology (fractional CMO, inbound-first, organic SEO), or when they want monthly flexibility rather than annual contracts.

### Which Directive alternative is best for early-stage SaaS?

PipeRocket Digital starts at $3,000/mo with monthly rolling terms and serves Seed through Series B. Kalungi is better if you need fractional CMO leadership to build the marketing function from scratch. Roketto works if you need a website rebuild alongside inbound marketing. All three serve the stage before Directive's typical client profile.

### Which alternative has the lowest verified starting price?

PipeRocket Digital ($3,000/mo, month-to-month) and KlientBoost (~$3,000/mo, industry-reported) have the lowest entry points on this list. Bay Leaf Digital's $3,999/mo Authority Builder requires a 6-month commitment. Refine Labs ($20,000/mo) and Kalungi ($25,000+ project) are the highest floor options.

### Do any of these agencies match Directive's attribution depth?

PipeRocket Digital and Refine Labs come closest to Directive's attribution rigour, through different frameworks. We report every channel against pipeline contribution and closed-won MRR. Refine Labs rebuilds your measurement model around dark-funnel demand generation. Both require buy-in from your sales team, comparable to adopting Directive's Customer Generation methodology.

### What should I ask an agency before signing as a Directive alternative?

Ask three things: can they show you a B2B SaaS case study with pipeline or closed-won MRR as the outcome metric (not just traffic or MQL volume); how do they attribute marketing spend to pipeline in your specific CRM; and what is the actual contract term and exit process. Price is rarely the problem — attribution gaps and contract rigidity are where most agency relationships break down.

### Is Directive Consulting worth the price for Series B+ companies?

For many Series B+ B2B SaaS companies, yes. Directive's Customer Generation framework, SaaS-exclusive focus, and paid-media depth justify the retainer if your team is ready to commit to the methodology. The alternatives above are better fits when you need a lower floor, a different strategic approach (organic-first, fractional CMO, or dark-funnel demand gen), or more contractual flexibility than Directive typically offers.

### How do I evaluate Clutch ratings for agencies with very few reviews?

Treat review count as a confidence interval, not just the rating itself. A 5.0 from 1 review (NoGood) or 4 reviews (Bay Leaf Digital) carries far less signal than a 4.9 from 402 reviews (KlientBoost). For agencies with zero Clutch reviews (Refine Labs, Kalungi, First Page Sage), ask for direct client references you can contact independently — don't rely on agency-curated testimonials alone.
