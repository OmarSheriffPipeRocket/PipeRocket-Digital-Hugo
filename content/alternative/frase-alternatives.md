Comparing the top 7 best Frase alternatives in 2026 includes 1. Surfer SEO, 2. Clearscope, 3. MarketMuse, 4. Scalenut, 5. NeuronWriter, 6. Search Atlas, 7. PageOptimizer Pro.

Surfer SEO is the market-leading NLP-driven content editor for teams that want the most granular SERP data and the widest user base; Clearscope suits enterprise teams and agencies that prioritize a clean, distraction-free editor with unlimited seats; MarketMuse is the choice for enterprise content strategists who need domain-level topical authority modeling; Scalenut is built for SMBs and agencies that want one end-to-end workflow from keyword cluster to AI draft; NeuronWriter targets solo SEOs and bloggers who need NLP-based scoring at the lowest price in the category; Search Atlas is the consolidation pick for agencies that want rank tracking, audits, keyword research, and AI writing under one subscription; and PageOptimizer Pro suits technical SEOs who want on-page recommendations grounded in controlled algorithm testing rather than SERP correlation alone.

Choosing the wrong content optimization tool costs more than the subscription: bad optimization guidance sends writers in the wrong direction before they type a sentence, and the wasted editing hours compound across every article in a content calendar. Each tool below was evaluated on content editor quality, AI writing output, SERP research depth, pricing transparency, and review signal from verified users. The primary reasons teams leave Frase are stiff AI writing output that requires heavy editing, a cluttered interface as features have accumulated, non-responsive billing support, and an inability to bring internal knowledge sources (Confluence, Notion, Google Drive) into the brief workflow.

## How We Chose These Tools?

Each tool on this list was evaluated using G2 reviews and qualitative signals from SEO practitioner discussions on Reddit, LinkedIn, and Quora. Pricing was verified directly from live product pages as of June 2026, and any field that could not be confirmed from a primary source is flagged in the relevant card below. The initial field was narrowed by eliminating tools that had no meaningful G2 presence or whose primary positioning did not overlap with Frase's core use case (SERP research, content optimization, AI-assisted writing).

## Why Look for a Frase Alternative?

The most consistent reason teams switch away from Frase comes down to AI output quality. Across G2 reviews and third-party coverage (sourced from youraisoft.com/frase-review/ and g2.com/products/frase-io/reviews), the dominant complaint is that Frase's AI writer produces stiff, formal output that requires heavy editing before it's usable. When the editing time on AI-generated drafts exceeds what writing from scratch would take, the core value proposition collapses.

The second cluster of complaints centers on interface clutter and support quality. As Frase has added features, reviewers describe the interface as increasingly hard to navigate. Separate from content quality, users report non-responsive customer support specifically on billing issues, including refund refusals past the trial deadline, which creates trust problems beyond the product experience.

Finally, a workflow limitation: Frase's briefs and optimization are grounded in publicly indexed content only. Teams that want to incorporate internal knowledge sources (Confluence pages, Notion wikis, Google Drive documents) into their briefs can't do it in Frase. For teams building content on proprietary internal expertise rather than public SERP data alone, that's a ceiling the tools above don't all share.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best Frase alternative?

Surfer SEO is the strongest like-for-like Frase alternative for most teams, with a larger SERP dataset, a more polished editor, and the widest G2 review base (537 reviews at 4.8/5) in this set. Clearscope is the best alternative for teams with multiple editors, and Scalenut is the best pick for teams that want GEO/AI search tracking built into the same workflow.

### Is Surfer SEO better than Frase?

For content teams that want the most granular NLP-term recommendations and the largest SERP data corpus, Surfer is the stronger tool. Its editor is more polished, its G2 rating (4.8/5, 537 reviews) exceeds Frase's review depth, and it's more widely referenced in SEO communities. The trade-off: Surfer is more expensive at comparable feature levels and requires a separate keyword research tool for a complete workflow.

### Does Clearscope have a free trial?

No free trial is listed on Clearscope's pricing page as of June 2026 (verify at clearscope.io/pricing before purchase). This is the main evaluation risk for buyers considering it: the $129/mo Essentials entry requires a paid commitment before firsthand testing. The 4.9/5 G2 satisfaction rating and published customer case studies (Webflow, Animalz, HubSpot) are the primary evaluation signals available without a trial.

### What is the cheapest Frase alternative?

NeuronWriter is the cheapest Frase alternative at $23/mo (Bronze plan, billed monthly), with NLP-based content scoring and SERP analysis comparable to the core Frase optimization layer. For teams that only need on-page optimization and not AI writing, PageOptimizer Pro starts at $40/mo and uses a controlled-testing methodology rather than SERP correlation.

### Which Frase alternative is best for agencies?

Search Atlas is the strongest agency pick for teams that want to consolidate multiple tool subscriptions (content optimization, rank tracking, keyword research, audits) with a white-label option at the Agency tier. For agencies focused purely on content quality and editor experience, Clearscope's unlimited-seat model delivers the lowest per-editor cost at scale.

### Can I replace Frase with a free tool?

Not fully. MarketMuse has a free plan (10 queries/mo), and NeuronWriter offers a 7-day trial without a stated credit card requirement, but no tool in this set delivers the full Frase experience for free. Most tools offer trial periods: Scalenut (7 days), NeuronWriter (7 days under Gold terms), and Search Atlas (7 days) are the most accessible low-risk evaluation options.
