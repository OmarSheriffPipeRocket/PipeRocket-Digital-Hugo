## How to choose

If you're already running Ahrefs and questioning the renewal, the right replacement depends on which job-to-be-done is actually unmet. Tracking? Wincher. Briefs? Clearscope. Volume + paid? Semrush. Hunting weak SERPs? LowFruits.

For most B2B SaaS teams under 30 marketers, the right move is rarely a full Ahrefs replacement - it's pairing a focused tool with the cheapest tier that still gives you backlink visibility.
