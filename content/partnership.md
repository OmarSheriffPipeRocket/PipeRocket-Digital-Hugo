## A partner program built for B2B SaaS, not a logo swap

PipeRocket Digital runs a partner program for people who already work with US B2B SaaS companies and want to make their referrals, integrations, or co-marketing actually move pipeline. Four tracks, each with a defined commercial model and a senior point of contact at PipeRocket. No tiered tier-of-tiers, no PDF certifications, no badge salad.

If you have a SaaS audience, a complementary service stack, or a product B2B SaaS marketing teams already use, one of the four tracks below is built for you.

## Four partnership tracks

### 1. Referral Partner

Best for: independent consultants, fractional CMOs, VC platform teams, SaaS angel investors, and operators who hear "we need a marketing agency" inside their network at least once a quarter.

- 15% revenue share on the first 12 months of every closed engagement you refer.
- Warm-intro support: we join your first call with the prospect if it helps the deal.
- Quarterly portfolio reviews: we share what is working across the agency book so you can position your referral confidently.
- No exclusivity: refer to other agencies if the fit is wrong. We would rather lose a referral than break a relationship.

### 2. Agency Partner

Best for: marketing, design, branding, or RevOps agencies that already serve B2B SaaS but do not run SaaS SEO, SaaS PPC, AEO/GEO, ABM, or programmatic SEO in-house.

- White-label or co-branded delivery on PipeRocket services your clients need but you do not offer.
- Sales-cycle support: we work inside your client's sales process, not parallel to it. Your account team owns the relationship.
- Co-marketing budget for a joint case study, webinar, or category report once we have shipped two engagements together.
- Margin structure agreed up front. No surprise mark-ups, no opaque revenue share.

### 3. Integration Partner

Best for: SaaS platforms whose product already lives inside the workflows our clients run, marketing automation, attribution, content, CRM enrichment, AI-search monitoring, and competitive intelligence tools.

- Joint go-to-market: a single named PipeRocket counterpart for every co-marketing, sales-play, and customer success motion.
- Featured in the PipeRocket tech stack on every client engagement where the integration is the right call. Not a generic listing, an actual default.
- Co-authored content: deep-cut technical guides, webinars, and live demos with our category-leading SaaS clients.
- Quarterly review against shared outcomes: how many joint accounts, how much pipeline influenced, how many renewals tied to the integration.

### 4. Content Partner

Best for: B2B SaaS publications, podcasters, newsletter operators, and category analysts with an audience that overlaps with US B2B SaaS marketing and growth leaders.

- Paid editorial sponsorships with full transparency on terms.
- Guest features: our practitioners author or appear on your show with category-specific takes (not generic "future of marketing" filler).
- Audience cross-pollination on category research we publish.
- First look at proprietary benchmarks, surveys, and case studies before they go public.

## What you can expect from us as a partner

- A named partner manager at PipeRocket who is empowered to make decisions, not pass them up a chain.
- A written partner agreement with the commercial model, attribution rules, and exit terms agreed before any pipeline moves.
- Quarterly reviews against the outcomes that matter for the track (referrals closed, joint clients shipped, integrations adopted, audience reached).
- The same delivery standards we hold ourselves to internally. If a partner engagement is failing, we say so first.

## What we expect from partners

- A real fit between your audience or capability and a B2B SaaS marketing buyer's actual need. We do not run rev-share farms.
- Direct, written communication. We do not run partnerships through forms or generic inboxes.
- No double-sided commission stacking on a single deal. One partner per engagement, agreed before the intro.
- An exit at any time. If the partnership stops producing value for either side, we end it cleanly and stay friendly.

## How to apply

The application path is intentionally simple.

1. Send a short note to **[partners@piperocket.digital](mailto:partners@piperocket.digital)** describing which of the four tracks you are interested in and the audience or capability you bring.
2. We respond within three business days with either a call invitation or a clear "not a fit right now" with reasons.
3. If we move forward, the partner agreement and onboarding kit are sent inside one week.

We onboard a small number of partners per quarter on each track to keep the relationships high-touch. If a track is at capacity we will say so and offer to reconnect in the next cycle.
