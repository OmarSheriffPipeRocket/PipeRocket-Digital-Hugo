## When you should not pick either

If your SEO team is one part-time marketer and your monthly content output is under four posts, both Ahrefs and Semrush will be 80% unused. Look at LowFruits or Wincher first and revisit a full suite when you have a dedicated SEO function.

## How most B2B SaaS teams actually use them

The pattern we see across 50+ B2B SaaS engagements: teams default to Ahrefs for the backlink and competitor research depth, and supplement with a focused content brief tool (Clearscope or Frase) rather than fighting Semrush's broader feature set.

When the team also runs paid search and wants one dataset, Semrush wins on consolidation alone. Don't pay for both - pick the one that aligns with how your team works today.
