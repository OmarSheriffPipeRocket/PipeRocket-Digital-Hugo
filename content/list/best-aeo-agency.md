SEO ranks pages on Google. AEO structures them for LLM retrieval. GEO builds brand authority so AI recommends you by default.

No. AI platforms cite pages that already rank on Google. Cut SEO and you weaken AEO at the same time. Run both together.

Citation frequency across LLMs, share of AI answer, branded AI mentions, AI referral traffic in GA4, and pipeline from AI sessions.
