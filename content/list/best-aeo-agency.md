## Frequently Asked Questions



### What is AEO?



Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring content so AI platforms like ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews can surface your brand as the direct answer — using semantic clarity, structured formatting, and entity signals for instant retrieval and citation.



### What is GEO?



Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of building brand authority so AI systems consistently cite, recommend, and prioritize your brand — using entity positioning, topical depth, and AI-aligned content to become the default source at scale.



### What’s the difference between AEO, GEO, and SEO?



SEO ranks your pages on Google. AEO structures those pages for LLM retrieval so AI platforms cite you in answers. GEO builds brand-level authority so AI systems recommend you by default. All three are layers of the same system — each one strengthens the others.

### How long does AEO take to show results in the US market?



Discovered Labs documented a client growing from 550 to 3,500+ AI-referred trials in seven weeks. Most B2B SaaS companies see initial citation movement in 4–8 weeks. Stable visibility across all platforms takes 3–6 months. GEO authority compounds over 6–12 months.

### Does AEO replace SEO?



No. AI platforms primarily cite pages that already rank on Google. Cutting SEO weakens AEO and GEO at the same time. The strongest programs in 2026 run SEO, AEO, and GEO together. SEO builds the foundation, AEO makes it AI-readable, GEO earns the recommendation.



### What metrics should a US AEO agency track?



Citation frequency across LLMs, share of AI answer for target queries, branded AI mentions, AI referral traffic in GA4, and pipeline from AI-referred sessions. Key tools: Conductor, Semrush AI Toolkit, Profound, and Scrunch. Reject agencies reporting only keyword rankings.



### Which AI platforms should US brands optimise for first?



Start with Google AI Overviews (13%+ of US desktop searches). Then ChatGPT (700M+ weekly users, highest B2B adoption). Then Perplexity (preferred by technical buyers and founders). Then Gemini. Each platform has different citation signals — a serious AEO agency addresses all four.



### How do I check if my US brand appears in AI answers today?



Search your top buyer queries in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews: “best [category] software,” “alternatives to [competitor],” and “[category] for [ICP].” Note where you appear, what AI says about you, and which competitors rank above you. This audit takes 15 minutes.
