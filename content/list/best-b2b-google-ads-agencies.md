## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes a Google Ads agency specialized in B2B different from general agencies?

B2B-specialized Google Ads agencies understand longer sales cycles, multiple decision-makers, and the importance of lead quality over volume. They build campaigns around pipeline metrics and revenue outcomes rather than clicks or form fills. These agencies typically integrate with CRM systems to track leads through the entire sales process. They also understand B2B buying behavior, including research phases and committee-based purchasing decisions.

### How much should a B2B company budget for Google Ads management?

Most B2B Google Ads agencies charge between $3,000 and $20,000 monthly for management fees, depending on ad spend levels and service scope. Enterprise engagements with complex attribution requirements often exceed $25,000 monthly. Beyond management fees, companies should budget for ad spend itself, which typically ranges from $5,000 to $100,000+ monthly depending on market competitiveness and growth goals.

### How long does it take to see results from B2B Google Ads campaigns?

Initial performance data typically emerges within 30 to 60 days of campaign launch. However, meaningful pipeline impact often takes 90 to 180 days due to longer B2B sales cycles. Agencies need time to gather conversion data, optimize targeting, and refine messaging based on lead quality feedback from sales teams. Companies should plan for a minimum six-month engagement to properly evaluate agency performance.

### Should B2B companies use Performance Max campaigns or stick with Search?

Most B2B companies benefit from a combination of Search and Performance Max campaigns. Search campaigns provide control over keyword targeting and work well for high-intent queries. Performance Max can expand reach across Google’s inventory but requires careful audience signals and conversion tracking. B2B-specialized agencies typically start with Search campaigns to establish baseline performance before testing Performance Max.

### How do B2B Google Ads agencies measure success beyond lead volume?

Sophisticated B2B agencies track metrics throughout the sales funnel, including cost per SQL, pipeline value generated, and closed revenue attributed to paid campaigns. They integrate with CRM systems like Salesforce or HubSpot to connect ad clicks to actual deals. This approach reveals true customer acquisition costs and helps optimize campaigns for revenue impact rather than surface-level metrics like cost per lead.
