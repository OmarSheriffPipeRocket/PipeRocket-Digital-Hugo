## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why should I choose a B2B-specialized SEO agency over a generalist?

Generalist agencies build frameworks around consumer industries like ecommerce and retail. B2B buying journeys involve multiple stakeholders, long research phases, and technical evaluations that generalists rarely understand. A B2B-specialized agency knows that a lead means a specific job title at a target company, not just a form submission. They build strategies for complex sales cycles and revenue attribution rather than traffic metrics alone.

### How long does it take to see results from B2B SEO?

Most B2B SEO engagements require three to six months before meaningful pipeline impact appears. Technical foundations and content production take time to index and rank. However, quick wins in technical SEO can show improvements within weeks. The longer timeline reflects B2B buying cycles, where prospects research extensively before engaging sales. Agencies promising immediate results likely focus on vanity metrics rather than qualified pipeline.

### What should I expect to pay for a quality B2B SEO agency?

Quality B2B SEO agencies typically start at $5,000 monthly for growth-stage companies. Enterprise engagements with comprehensive strategy and dedicated teams range from $15,000 to $50,000 monthly. Pricing reflects team expertise, scope of services, and expected business impact. Agencies charging significantly below market rates often lack B2B specialization or rely on junior staff and templated approaches that fail complex organizations.

### How do I measure ROI from B2B SEO investment?

Effective B2B SEO measurement goes beyond traffic and rankings to track pipeline contribution. Work with your agency to establish attribution models connecting organic traffic to SQLs and closed revenue. Track metrics like organic-sourced pipeline value, cost per SQL from organic channels, and organic influence on deals. The best agencies help build dashboards showing direct revenue impact rather than just visibility metrics.

### What questions should I ask before hiring a B2B SEO agency?

Ask about their experience with your specific industry and sales cycle length. Request case studies showing pipeline impact, not just traffic growth. Understand their approach to technical SEO versus content and how they balance both. Clarify reporting frequency and what metrics they prioritize. Finally, ask how they handle communication and who your day-to-day contact will be throughout the engagement.
