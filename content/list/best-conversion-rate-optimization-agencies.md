## How We Chose These Conversion Rate Optimization Agencies?

We cross-referenced Clutch ratings and written reviews, G2 profiles, and practitioner discussions on Reddit (r/analytics, r/SaaS, r/marketing) and LinkedIn to build a shortlist of active agencies with verifiable B2B SaaS work. Agencies were then checked for active websites, named SaaS clients, and publicly confirmed pricing floors. Ecommerce-only shops and agencies on our standing blocklist were excluded outright.

The two criteria that carried the most weight for this B2B SaaS-focused list: documented SaaS funnel experience (trial/demo flows, pricing page testing, PLG onboarding) and independent third-party review volume. Self-published testimonials were noted but not treated as equivalent to verified third-party reviews.

## FAQs

### What is conversion rate optimization (CRO)?

CRO is the practice of increasing the percentage of visitors who take a desired action: signing up for a trial, requesting a demo, or upgrading to a paid plan. It uses A/B testing, UX research, and data analysis.

### What makes CRO agencies different for B2B SaaS vs ecommerce?

B2B SaaS CRO focuses on trial sign-ups, demo requests, and pricing-page decisions: longer buying cycles, multiple stakeholders, and lower traffic volumes that require longer test runtimes than ecommerce checkout flows.

### How much do CRO agencies charge?

Pure-play CRO firms typically start at $7,500 to $10,000 per month. High-volume enterprise programs like CRO Metrics can run $200,000 per year. Integrated retainers like PipeRocket start at $3,000/mo but bundle CRO with SEO and paid media.

### How do I know if my SaaS company is ready for a CRO agency?

You need enough traffic to reach statistical significance in tests: typically 1,000 or more unique monthly visitors to the specific page being tested. Below that, tests take months to complete and results become unreliable.

### Should I hire a pure-play CRO agency or an integrated agency?

Pure-play firms (Conversion, Invesp, CRO Metrics, Speero) are better for teams that want a dedicated experimentation program. Integrated agencies (PipeRocket, Aimers) work better when CRO is one component of a broader acquisition strategy and you want one team accountable for pipeline outcomes.

### Are CRO agencies worth it for Series A SaaS?

Yes, if you have enough traffic and a clear conversion bottleneck. The most common mistake at Series A is hiring a CRO agency before the acquisition funnel generates enough volume to test at meaningful speed.

### What should I ask a CRO agency before signing?

Ask for SaaS-specific case studies (not just ecommerce), their minimum traffic requirements for testing, how they measure success beyond conversion rate (MQL quality, CAC), and what their test velocity looks like for a team at your traffic level.
