## The challenge

In a category where buyers Google "[category] vs [incumbent]" before they even open a sales call, organic visibility is table stakes. CyberSierra needed to show up — and rank — for the queries that lead to demos.

## Our approach

Topical authority programme: compliance frameworks, control mappings, audit prep playbooks. Each cluster anchored by a BOFU comparison or alternative page. Technical SEO foundations rebuilt in parallel to make sure the content could compound.

Trust-signal hygiene throughout: clear authorship, citations, schema, and conversion paths that match how compliance buyers actually evaluate vendors.

## The results

**Organic traffic grew 279%** over the engagement. The wins came from BOFU and comparison queries — the ones most directly tied to demo conversion in a high-trust category.
