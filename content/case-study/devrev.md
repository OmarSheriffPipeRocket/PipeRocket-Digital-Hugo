## About DevRev

DevRev is a unified platform for product, support, and CRM — built for B2B SaaS teams that don't want three disconnected tools handling the same customer. The category is dense: PMs, support leads, and revops all evaluate from different angles.

## The challenge

Organic was a fraction of where it needed to be. The team had aggressive growth targets and a strong product, but the search footprint wasn't matching the brand's ambition.

## Our approach

Six months. Topical authority programme around DevRev's three buyer personas (product, support, CRM), backed by BOFU comparison pages targeting the specific incumbents DevRev was displacing.

Technical hygiene first. Then content at pace. Then internal linking to compound what was already ranking.

## The results

**Organic traffic grew 127% in six months** — better than doubling. Crucially, the new traffic was concentrated in BOFU and high-intent mid-funnel pages, not just informational top-of-funnel.
