On documented third-party proof, Directive is ahead: 4.8 on Clutch across 56 reviews, plus two Inc. 5000 listings, is a deep public trail. Refine Labs counters on demand-creation depth and enterprise positioning — it helped define the modern demand-gen playbook and serves a $30M+ ARR ICP that Directive's lower floor isn't exclusively built for. But weigh two material facts: Refine Labs has no SEO offering, and its founder, whose personal brand powered the firm's early demand engine, fully exited in July 2025. Decide which form of evidence and which motion matter more to you.
