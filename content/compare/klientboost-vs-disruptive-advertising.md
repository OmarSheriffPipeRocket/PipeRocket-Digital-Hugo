## Strengths & tradeoffs

Both are excellent at the core job — paid media that converts, backed by CRO. The honest differences are at the edges, and each side wins some rows.

| Axis | KlientBoost | Disruptive Advertising |
|---|---|---|
| **Public proof** | 4.9 on Clutch across 402 reviews; 4.8 on G2 across 381 | 4.8 on Clutch across 365 reviews — strong, slightly fewer |
| **Tenure** | Founded 2015 | Founded 2012 — three years longer in market |
| **CRO** | Signature CRO and aggressive landing-page testing | CRO offered, but PPC is the headline service |
| **Guarantee** | No publicly advertised performance guarantee | Results-based guarantee — "if we don't deliver, you don't pay" |
| **Industry breadth** | Focused on B2B SaaS and eCommerce | B2B + B2C across 10+ verticals, SMB to enterprise |
| **Brand pedigree** | Airbnb, Le Tote, Thinkific, AdRoll | Scotts Miracle-Gro, Xactware, BookBaby, Nani Swimwear |
| **Organic SEO** | Partial — paid is the lead channel | Offered — but still paid-first |

On public review volume and rating, KlientBoost is narrowly ahead — 402 Clutch reviews at 4.9, plus a large G2 trail. Disruptive Advertising counters with three more years in market, a larger headcount, a broader industry footprint, and a results-based guarantee that KlientBoost doesn't advertise. Both hold Google Premier Partner status, so the choice is less about quality and more about fit: focused CRO-led paid for SaaS and eCommerce, or broader full-funnel performance with a guarantee.
