    SE Ranking and Semrush are both full-stack SEO platforms that cover rank
    tracking, keyword research, site audits, and backlink analysis. The honest
    split comes down to scale versus cost. Semrush carries the largest keyword

    research. SE Ranking is a leaner, lower-priced alternative with more
    generous project and crawl limits at its entry tier, a no-card 14-day trial,
    and white-label reporting available without upgrading to a mid-tier plan.

## Strengths & tradeoffs

Both tools cover the core SEO job well. The differences that matter are at the edges, and each side wins meaningful rows.

| Axis | SE Ranking | Semrush |
|---|---|---|
| **Keyword database** | 5.4 billion keywords | 27.9 billion keywords (roughly 5x larger) |
| **Backlink database** | 2.9 trillion links | 43 trillion links (roughly 15x larger) |
| **Entry project limits** | 10 projects, 250k pages/mo audit on Core | 5 projects, 100k pages/mo audit on Pro |
| **Entry price (annual)** | $103.20/mo (Core) | $117.33/mo (Classic Pro) |
| **White-label access** | Core plan + Agency Pack add-on ($69/mo annual) | Guru plan minimum ($208.33/mo annual) |
| **Outreach CRM** | Not available | Built-in link-building prospect and outreach tool |
| **Google Shopping ads** | Not available | Included in PPC research |
| **Local SEO** | Built-in (Google Maps tracking included) | Separate add-on (from $30/mo) |
| **AI visibility (GEO)** | Add-on at $89/mo; one 60-day test reported unreliable results | Bundled in Semrush One; #1 in 6 G2 Spring 2026 categories |
| **Historical data** | From approximately 2020 | From 2012 |
| **Free trial** | 14 days, no credit card | 7 days |
| **G2 rating and ease of use** | 4.8/5; ease of use 9.3/10 | 4.5/5; ease of use 8.4/10 |
| **Pricing complexity** | Simpler tiered model; one plan track | Two parallel tracks (Classic + Semrush One) plus 6+ add-on toolkits; cost can exceed $500/mo at scale |
