## Strengths & tradeoffs

Both are credible enterprise ABM platforms and excel at the core job (turning account signals into a prioritized motion). The honest differences are at the edges, and each side wins some rows.

| Axis | 6sense | Demandbase |
|---|---|---|
| **Predictive intent** | Core differentiator: buying-stage prediction (Revenue AI) | Offered, but breadth is the lead story, not predictive depth |
| **De-anonymization** | Core strength: surfaces accounts before they fill a form | Available within account intelligence, not the headline feature |
| **B2B advertising** | No native DSP; relies on a separate ad stack | Native B2B DSP (Self-Serve + Managed) inside the platform |
| **Platform breadth** | Focused on intent and prioritization | All-in-one: data, contacts, engagement, intent, advertising |
| **Pricing model** | Custom; reported multi-year minimums (unverified) | Custom; DSP ad-spend minimums add cost (unverified) |
| **Onboarding** | Signal volume rewards teams with process to action it | Breadth creates a learning curve and admin overhead |
| **Free entry** | Free Sales Intelligence tier to trial | No free plan |
