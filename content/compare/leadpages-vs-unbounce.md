  intro: >-
    Leadpages and Unbounce are two of the longest-running landing page
    builders on the market, both founded over a decade ago and both now
    layering AI optimization on top of their drag-and-drop cores. The
    honest split comes down to traffic and testing access: Leadpages has
    no visitor caps and unlocks A/B testing at $99/mo, while Unbounce
    enforces monthly visitor limits and gates A/B testing behind a higher
    tier, but offers a longer no-card free trial and a larger pool of
    independently verified case studies.

        Leadpages is the stronger pick. The AI auto-optimization loop on

        independently verified brand-name case studies to benchmark
        conversion benchmarks, Unbounce is the more proven pick. Its
        Smart Traffic model also cites a **2 billion conversion** training
        dataset versus a smaller stated figure from Leadpages.

faqs:
  - q: "What is the difference between Leadpages and Unbounce?"
    a: >-
      Both are landing page builders with AI routing and A/B testing, but
      they differ on traffic and testing access. Leadpages has no visitor
      caps and unlocks A/B testing at $99/mo, plus built-in heatmaps from
      $199/mo. Unbounce enforces monthly visitor limits with overage fees,
      gates A/B testing at $149/mo, and has no native heatmaps, but
      offers a 14-day no-card trial and more independently verified
      brand-name case studies.
  - q: "Is Leadpages better than Unbounce?"
    a: >-
      Neither is better in the abstract. Leadpages is the stronger pick
      for high-traffic campaigns, teams that want A/B testing from a
      lower price point, and marketers who need heatmap data without
      paying for a third-party tool. Unbounce is the stronger pick for
      agencies managing multiple client domains, teams that want a longer
      no-card evaluation period, and buyers who weight a larger
      independently verified case study library. The right choice depends
      on your traffic volume, feature priority, and agency versus in-house
      context.
  - q: "Which is cheaper, Leadpages or Unbounce?"
    a: >-
      Both have a practical entry at $99/mo (Leadpages Grow with A/B
      testing; Unbounce Build without A/B testing). For the same feature
      set including A/B testing, Leadpages is cheaper: $99/mo versus
      $149/mo on Unbounce. For Smart Traffic AI, Leadpages is also cheaper:
      $199/mo versus $249/mo on Unbounce. Unbounce offers a slightly
      higher annual discount (25% versus 20%), so annual pricing closes
      some of the gap. Verify current pricing live before purchase.
  - q: "Does Unbounce have built-in heatmaps?"
    a: >-
      No. Unbounce does not include native heatmaps on any plan. Teams
      that want click and scroll data alongside Unbounce need to connect
      an external tool such as Hotjar. Leadpages includes built-in
      heatmaps from its Optimize plan ($199/mo).
  - q: "Does Leadpages have visitor traffic limits like Unbounce?"
    a: >-
      No. Leadpages does not cap monthly visitors on any of its CRO plans.
      Unbounce applies monthly visitor caps ranging from 500 on Starter
      to 50,000 on Optimize, with overage fees if the limit is exceeded.
      For campaigns with high or seasonal traffic spikes, Leadpages
      removes the budget risk of overage charges.
  - q: "Which offers a better free trial, Leadpages or Unbounce?"
    a: >-
      Unbounce offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required,
      while Leadpages offers a 7-day trial that requires a credit card at
      signup with no charge until day 7. Unbounce's trial is longer and
      lower friction. Leadpages' trial does include full plan access for
      the 7 days. Verify current trial terms on each vendor's site before
      signing up, as these have changed in the past.

## Strengths & tradeoffs

Both tools build landing pages competently, and both have layered AI routing on top. The differences sit in traffic handling, where testing unlocks, and what each includes natively versus what you have to pay extra for elsewhere.

| Axis | Leadpages | Unbounce |
|---|---|---|
| **Visitor traffic** | No caps on any plan; no overage risk | Capped 500 to 50,000/mo by tier; overages apply if exceeded |
| **A/B testing access** | Manual A/B from Grow ($99/mo) | Requires Experiment ($149/mo); an extra $50/mo versus Leadpages |
| **Smart Traffic AI** | From Optimize ($199/mo) | From Optimize ($249/mo); claims 2B-conversion training dataset |
| **Auto-optimization loop** | Continuous auto-loop on Scale ($399/mo); no Unbounce equivalent | No automated testing loop; manual A/B only |
| **Heatmaps** | Built in from Optimize ($199/mo) | Not native; requires external tool (e.g. Hotjar) |
| **Lead enrichment** | IP enrichment built into CRO plans (volume varies by tier) | Not offered natively |
| **Free trial** | 7 days, full access, card required | 14 days, no card required; lower friction to evaluate |
| **Annual discount** | 20% | 25%; slightly better for annual buyers |
| **Social proof** | Case study company names not independently verifiable | Going.com, Campaign Monitor, Later all on Unbounce's published case studies with named metrics |
| **Agency tooling** | Scale plan (30 domains, $399/mo) | Dedicated Agency plan (10+ domains, custom pricing) |
| **Ownership stability** | Redbrick portfolio since 2020; roadmap depends on parent priorities | Crest Rock Partners; merged with Insightly (2024); CRM integration may shift roadmap |
