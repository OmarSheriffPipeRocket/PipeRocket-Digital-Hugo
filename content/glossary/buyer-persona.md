## Buyer persona vs ICP

ICP describes **the company**. Buyer persona describes **the person inside that company who buys, evaluates, or champions the product**. Most B2B SaaS deals involve 4-7 personas in the buying committee - economic buyer, end user, technical evaluator, finance signoff, security/legal - each with different objections.

## What a useful persona looks like

- The exact title (not "marketer" but "Head of Demand Generation at $5M-$50M ARR B2B SaaS")
- Three goals they're judged on
- Three objections that kill deals at this persona
- Where they consume content (LinkedIn? Slack communities? Newsletters?)
- The signal that says they're in-market
