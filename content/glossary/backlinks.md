## What makes a backlink valuable for B2B SaaS

Most agencies grade by domain rating. That's a proxy. The questions that actually matter:

1. Do your buyers read this publication?
2. Does the linking page get organic traffic in your category?
3. Is the link inside existing content that already ranks, or a new guest post on a low-traffic blog?
4. Will AI models like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews cite this source?

A DR-80 link from a lifestyle blog with no SaaS audience is worth less than a DR-55 link from an industry publication your buyers actually read.

## Red flags

- Quotas measured in number of links per month rather than placement quality
- "Editorial" placements that turn out to be brand-new guest posts on freshly created blogs
- Templated outreach pitches you can recognise in your spam folder
